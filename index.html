True Words

By Valeria Herrera

What Is the Library?
Is quiet, It's empty, It smells good like old books
A charming recommendation
The book can change a reader's life

It asks a question,
Where is the library?
A place where you can find a lot of books
Where you sit all morning

They have challenges
...the book I am reading
With sentences found from the forest
As we are speaking

They might whisper secrets
I went, I read, I stayed
